Shipping Policy

Effective Date: 14 July 2024

Welcome to Kids Fashion Store ("we," "our," "us"). This Shipping Policy describes the terms and conditions under which we offer shipping services for our products purchased through our website, https://kidsfashionstore.ua (the "Site").

1. Shipping Areas

We offer shipping to addresses across Ukraine and selected countries in Europe using Nova Post (Нова Пошта). Please note that shipping availability, times, and costs may vary depending on the destination and are subject to change.

2. Shipping Times

Shipping times will vary based on the destination:

  • Ukraine: Delivery times typically range from 1 to 3 business days.
  • Europe: Delivery times to European countries may vary and will be specified during checkout.

Please note that delivery times are estimates and are not guaranteed. Factors such as customs processing, weather conditions, and holidays may affect delivery times.

3. Shipping Costs

  • Shipping costs are calculated based on the delivery address and the size/weight of the package. Costs may vary as follows:
    • Big Cities: Shipping costs may be lower.
    • Small Villages: Shipping costs may be higher due to longer transit times and additional handling fees.
  • Shipping costs will be calculated and displayed during the checkout process. Customers are responsible for all shipping costs, which will be added to the total order amount.

4. Order Processing

Orders are typically processed and shipped within 1 to 2 business days of payment confirmation. Once your order has shipped, you will receive a shipping confirmation email with tracking information.

5. Tracking Information

Once your order has shipped, you can track your package using the tracking number provided in your shipping confirmation email or by logging into your account on our website.

6. Customs and Duties

For international shipments outside of Ukraine, customs duties, taxes, and fees may apply. These charges are the responsibility of the recipient and will be collected upon delivery by the local customs authority. Customs policies vary widely from country to country, so please contact your local customs office for more information.

7. Lost or Damaged Shipments

We are not responsible for lost or damaged shipments. However, if your package is lost or damaged during transit, please contact us immediately at contact@kidsfashionstore.ua or +380 68 901 6787 so that we can assist you in filing a claim with the shipping carrier.

8. Address Accuracy

It is the customer's responsibility to provide accurate and complete shipping information. We are not responsible for orders shipped to incorrect or incomplete addresses provided by the customer. Please review your shipping address carefully before submitting your order.

9. Shipping Restrictions

We reserve the right to refuse orders for any reason, including shipping restrictions imposed by our shipping carriers.

10. Contact Information

If you have any questions about our Shipping Policy, please contact us at contact@kidsfashionstore.ua or +380 68 901 6787.

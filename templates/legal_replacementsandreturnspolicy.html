Shipping Policy

Effective Date: 14 July 2024

Thank you for shopping at Kids Fashion Store. We want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If you are not entirely satisfied with your purchase, we're here to help.

1. Eligibility

To be eligible for a replacement or return, your item must be unused and in the same condition that you received it. It must also be in the original packaging.

2. Timeline

You have one day from the date you receive your order (either by picking it up from the post office or receiving it via delivery) to initiate a replacement or return request.

3. How to Initiate a Replacement or Return

To initiate a replacement or return, please follow these steps:

  • Contact our Customer Service team at customerservice@kidsfashionstore.ua within the one-day timeframe to notify us of your request.
  • Provide your order number and details about the item you wish to replace or return.
  • Our Customer Service team will guide you through the process and provide you with a return shipping label (if applicable).

4. Conditions of Replacement or Return

  • Items must be unused and in the same condition that you received them.
  • Items must be in their original packaging.
  • Items not eligible for replacement or return include:
    • Items that are not in their original condition, damaged, or missing parts for reasons not due to our error.
    • Items returned more than one day after delivery.

5. Refunds or Replacements

Once your return is received and inspected, we will send you an email to notify you that we have received your returned item. We will also notify you of the approval or rejection of your replacement or refund.

  • If approved, replacements will be processed based on product availability. If the same product is not available, a similar product of equal value may be offered.
  • If approved, refunds will be processed to the original method of payment within 2 business days.

6. Shipping Costs

Shipping costs for returns are non-refundable. If you receive a replacement, the cost of return shipping will be deducted from your refund.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions about our Replacement and Return Policy, please contact us at customerservice@kidsfashionstore.ua.

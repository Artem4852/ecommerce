How to Select the Right Shoe Size for Your Child

Choosing the right shoe size for your child is essential for their comfort and foot health. Follow these simple steps to ensure you get the perfect fit.

StepByStep Instructions:

1. Prepare the Materials

  • A piece of paper
  • A pencil
  • A ruler

2. Trace Your Child’s Foot

  • Place the piece of paper on a flat, hard surface.
  • Have your child stand barefoot on the paper.
  • Ensure your child’s foot is flat and their weight is evenly distributed.
  • Using the pencil, carefully trace around your child’s foot. Try to keep the pencil as vertical as possible to get an accurate outline.

3. Measure the Foot Length

  • Remove the paper and use the ruler to measure the longest distance from the heel to the tip of the longest toe.
  • This measurement is your child’s foot length.

4. Compare with Insole Lengths

  • On our shop, each shoe size includes the length of the insole.
  • Compare your child’s foot length to the insole lengths provided.
  • Choose the shoe size where the insole length is slightly longer than your child’s foot length, allowing for some growing room.

Additional Tips:

  • Measure Both Feet: Sometimes one foot is slightly larger than the other. Use the measurement of the larger foot to ensure comfort.
  • Consider Growth: Kids' feet grow quickly, so it’s a good idea to leave a little extra room (about one centimeter) for growth.
  • Try Shoes On: If possible, have your child try on the shoes and walk around to ensure they feel comfortable.
  • The shoes should have enough room at the toes and fit snugly around the heels.

Example:

  • If your child’s foot length is 18 cm, look for a shoe size with an insole length of around 18.5 - 19 cm.

By following these steps, you can confidently select the correct shoe size for your child, ensuring they have comfortable and well fitting shoes. Happy Shopping!

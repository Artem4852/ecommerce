{{ _('How to Select the Right Shoe Size for Your Child') }}

{{ _('Choosing the right shoe size for your child is essential for their comfort and foot health. Follow these simple steps to ensure you get the perfect fit.') }}

{{ _('Step-by-step Instructions') }}:

1. {{ _('Prepare the Materials') }}

  • {{ _('A piece of paper') }}
  • {{ _('A pencil') }}
  • {{ _('A ruler') }}

2. {{ _('Trace Your Child’s Foot') }}

  • {{ _('Place the piece of paper on a flat, hard surface.') }}
  • {{ _('Have your child stand barefoot on the paper.') }}
  • {{ _('Ensure your child’s foot is flat and their weight is evenly distributed.') }}
  • {{ _('Using the pencil, carefully trace around your child’s foot. Try to keep the pencil as vertical as possible to get an accurate outline.') }}

3. {{ _('Measure the Foot Length') }}

  • {{ _('Remove the paper and use the ruler to measure the longest distance from the heel to the tip of the longest toe.') }}
  • {{ _('This measurement is your child’s foot length.') }}

4. {{ _('Compare with Insole Lengths') }}

  • {{ _('On our shop, each shoe size includes the length of the insole.') }}
  • {{ _('Compare your child’s foot length to the insole lengths provided.') }}
  • {{ _('Choose the shoe size where the insole length is slightly longer than your child’s foot length, allowing for some growing room.') }}

{{ _('Additional Tips') }}:

  • {{ _('Measure Both Feet: Sometimes one foot is slightly larger than the other. Use the measurement of the larger foot to ensure comfort') }}.
  • {{ _('Consider Growth: Kids\' feet grow quickly, so it’s a good idea to leave a little extra room (about one centimeter) for growth') }}.
  • {{ _('Try Shoes On: If possible, have your child try on the shoes and walk around to ensure they feel comfortable') }}.
  • {{ _('The shoes should have enough room at the toes and fit snugly around the heels') }}.

{{ _('Example') }}:

  • {{ _('If your child’s foot length is 18 cm, look for a shoe size with an insole length of around 18.5 - 19 cm') }}.

{{ _('By following these steps, you can confidently select the correct shoe size for your child, ensuring they have comfortable and well fitting shoes. Happy Shopping!') }}
